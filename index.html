Introduction

Launchpad Kerala 2024, a collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn, is a prestigious job fair aimed at creating opportunities for engineering students and graduates in Kerala. The event features a rigorous assessment process including technical challenges and online tests to evaluate candidates' skills. Selected candidates can choose interview locations in Trivandrum, Kochi, or Kozhikode, simplifying the hiring process for companies. The event offers access to a diverse talent pool from across Kerala, providing companies with top-tier candidates for various roles.
